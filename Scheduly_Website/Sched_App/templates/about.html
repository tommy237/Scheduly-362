A B O U T

Scheduly is a scheduling application where users can create simple tasks and events at ease without a complicated outlook on their daily lives. A social media application where users can interact and plan accordingly for hangouts, interviews, occasional events, and much more!

Scheduly aims to provide better time management and social update support for users who should be up-to-date with their mutual friends, or accomodate their tasks and productivity under an organized timeline.

There are various features to be implemented for Scheduly, so that users are provided better and accessible resources when it comes to navigating, managing, and reinforcing their daily lives.
